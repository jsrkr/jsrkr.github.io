# Jheelum Sarkar

## About Me

I am a PhD candidate in Economics at American University, Washington, DC. My research lies at the intersection of labor, gender, development, and environmental economics. I use applied econometrics and machine learning techniques to study issues such as telework, digital inclusion, and climate shocks.
