Dashboard Notes

Proxies

The dashboard distinguishes direct measurement from proxy measurement because digital life is not observed equally well across concepts and geographies.

GenAI search attention

Built from state Google Trends interest in a small GenAI keyword basket. It measures relative search attention, not direct usage or minutes.

Digital dating search attention

Built from a dating keyword basket. Relative search attention, not direct dating-app usage.

Digital distraction

Measured from ATUS national time-use categories such as computer leisure, games, and television or movies. Stronger for minutes, weaker for precise state-year breakdowns.

Face-to-face social interaction

Measured from ATUS socializing and communicating categories. This is the cleaner benchmark for in-person social time, but version 1 uses national rather than precise state-nowcast values.

Remote-work intensity

Observed more directly than the digital-social margin. In version 1 it anchors one part of the broader digital-life mechanism rather than the whole story.
